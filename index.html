Our Story

Started in 1984, by our great grandfather Marcelo Grasso, his dream was to aspire and share the passion and love of his South Western French cuisine and cooking. .

The integrity of family and home cooking, invited many to host birthdays, parties and even full-on grand weddings. One notable moment was a party held by Princess Alix of Luxembourg with her spouse Antoine Maria Joachim Lamoral de Ligne.

Menu

Meet Our Chef

Marcelo Grasso is the head chef of our kitchen. A great leader, organizator and chef all around, Marcelo is also the owner of the restaurant, handling the many transactions and deicisions of everything. The menu was personally made by him.

In the kitchen, there is also his daughter, Shaona and his son, Luigi. With many waiters and chefs from all around of France, La Croux offers a very comfortable but dedicated and cooperative kitchen atmosphere..
